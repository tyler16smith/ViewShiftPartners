Investments

ViewShift does not currently make capital investments.

While we intend to raise ViewShift Partners Fund I and make investments ourselves, we have decided to rely on our network of investors to connect our startups with the funding they need.

Fund I Focus

  • Series A-B
  • Founders Fixated on Mission
  • Tech-Enabled Disruption
  • "Double Bottom Line" or Equal Consideration for Social Impact

Fund I will maintain the standard 2% management fee but will not take any carry from exited investments. 100% of realized investments will be returned to our Limited Partners.
