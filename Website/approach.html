Our Approach





Decision to Partner

Any startup working to make the world a better place has an open invitation to pitch to the ViewShift team. Meetings are held on a weekly basis and are done remotely. During a pitch, we'll want to hear about your story, mission, and progress.


If we feel confident both in the management team's ability to execute and our own ability to add meaningful value, we'll designate a Partner to be your advisor.






Advisory and Strategic Assistance

Our team is full of exceptionally talented individuals with expertise in a wide range of fields. Your Partner will have certain experiences that make them especially useful to you, but through them, you have access to our entire team as needed. If they lack a certain skill that you need, they will loop in a member of our team that can help.


The depth and frequency of our involvement is completely up to the management team's decision. We're not here to control or run your company. Whether you need us every other quarter or every other day, we can be flexible.


We are dedicated to doing whatever it takes to take you to the next level, whether that means giving feedback on product or listening in on sales calls or helping source/interview candidates.






Connection to Capital

Our team has experience investing in every stage, so when it's time for you to bring in an investor, we will walk you through the process and teach you how to speak their language.


We also have an extensive network of investors that we can leverage to make warm introductions for you. If the right investor for you isn't someone we know right now, we'll help you find them.
